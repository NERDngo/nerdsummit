NERD Summit 2023

Sponsorship Information

 

NERD Summit is approaching and we are excited to tell you about our great sponsorship opportunities. Your support is much appreciated to help make this fantastic community event happen.

Each Summit, every year since 2014, several hundred information technology professionals descend on NERD Summit.

NERD Summit is a free mini-conference centered on current and emerging languages and techniques in web development, UX, AI, and much more.

NERD Summit features knowledgeable speakers, educational workshops; an entertaining and informative keynote speaker; free breakfast and lunch; and invaluable opportunities for attendees to network with other developers in and around New England.

NERD Summit has been an incredible success thanks to excellent presenters, generous sponsors, and an enthusiastic community.

Sponsor packages are designed to help your organization with brand awareness, talent recruitment, and networking with potential partners and customers!
